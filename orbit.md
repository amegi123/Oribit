# Orbit Electronics Ethiopia

## About Us

Orbit Electronics Ethiopia is a leading electronics distributor committed to providing high-quality and innovative home appliances and electronic products to the Ethiopian market.

Before selecting Orbit as our flagship brand, we spent more than 15 years in the electronics industry. This experience gave us extensive knowledge and understanding of various electronics brands, including their strengths and weaknesses.

After conducting thorough research and detailed market analysis, we chose to exclusively work with Orbit Electronics products. The brand stands out for its exceptional quality, modern design, product availability, and continuous innovation. These qualities have made Orbit the brand we proudly represent in Ethiopia.
